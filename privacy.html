Privacy Policy

Byrd Family Finance

Last Updated: October 30, 2025

What We Collect

We collect financial data through Plaid: bank account balances, transaction history (past 2 years), and account details.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely for personal household finance management: balance display, spending analysis, smart grocery lists, savings goals tracking, budget monitoring, and notifications.

How We Protect Your Data

All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Data stored on private server, not public cloud. Multi-factor authentication required. Regular security audits.

Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with any third parties. Your financial data stays within our private household infrastructure.

Your Rights

You can access, export, delete, or disconnect your accounts at any time through the application settings.
